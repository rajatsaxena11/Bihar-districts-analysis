The 10 metrics obtained from the dataset are:

Female_to_Male_Ratio =                         female population/male population
Infants_per_Household=                         no. of infants/no. of households
Ratio_of_Underpriviledged_Population=          (sum of SC and ST population)/total population
Literacy_rate=                                 literate population/total population
Male_Literacy_rate=                            literate male population/male population
Female_Literacy_rate=                          literate female population/female population
Working_population_ratio=                      working population/total population
Male_Working_population_ratio=                 working male population/male population
Female_Working_population_ratio=               working female population/female population
Ratio_of_agriculture_to_industrial_households= agriculture population/industrial population

From the above 10 metrics, 3 other indices are derived

Social parameter=            (1/ Ratio_of_Underpriviledged_Population)*(1/ Infants_per_Household)

Economic parameter=          (Literacy_rate * Working_population_ratio )/ Ratio_of_agriculture_to_industrial_households
				 	
Women upliftment parameter=  Female_to_Male_Ratio * (Female_Literacy_rate/ Male_Literacy_rate)* (Female_Working_population_ratio/ Male_Working_population_ratio)

Based on the decreasing values of above 3 indices, the files o1 o2 and o3 are prepared.
